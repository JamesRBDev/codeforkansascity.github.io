SCHEDULE

Overview

Saturday you will:

  • Get your team together
  • Define the problem and find the best solution
  • Start on the design and coding of your solution

Sunday you will:

  • Create a roadmap for the project
  • Present your work completed

In July teams will present their progress, to date, at an all-hands meeting. Date TBD.

In October/November final presentations will be made along with a shared celebration. Date TBD.

Join local designers, developers, doers and thinkers with a passion for making local government work better through technology.

Details

Saturday January 12th, 2019

  • 9:00 - Registration - Arrive and get checked in
  • 9:30 - Initial Talks
  • 9:45 - Ice Breaker
  • 10:00 - Project Pitches - 5 minutes for each team leader to pitch their project, plus open pitches
  • 10:45 Form Teams - This is the opportunity for those that are not on a team to find a team they can join.
  • 11:15 Start Work - Teams will take evaluate the skills they have, start discussing, designing and planning.
  • 12:30 - Working lunch provided
  • 3:00pm or so - Chiefs fans exit
  • 4:00pm - Wrap up for day

Sunday January 13th, 2019

  • 9:00am - Open hacking & dev
  • 12:00pm - Working lunch provided
  • 3:00pm - Team presentations & demos
  • 4:00pm - Wrap up & next steps
